Australia is in the grip of its worst wildfire season on record. Earth-sized exoplanet ‘habitable zone’ is one of closest yet to our world

Astronomers have discovered a world only a little bit bigger than Earth, whirling around a bright star about 31 parsecs from our planet. The world, known as TOI 700 d, orbits in its star’s ‘habitable zone’ — the region in which liquid water could exist. Astronomers know of only a handful of such worlds. Read More...
